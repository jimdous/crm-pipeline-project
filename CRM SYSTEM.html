Each query answers a real business question. This is what Solutions Engineering thinking looks like.
Query 1 — total active pipeline value
Business question: How much revenue potential is currently in the pipeline?
SELECT SUM(estimated_deal_value) AS total_pipeline FROM leads WHERE outcome = 'Open';

Query 2 — best performing lead source
Business question: Where should we increase marketing spend?
SELECT lead_source, COUNT(*) AS total_leads, COUNT(CASE WHEN outcome='Won' THEN 1 END) AS closed_won FROM leads GROUP BY lead_source ORDER BY closed_won DESC;
Top source
Query 3 — conversion rate by lead source
Business question: Which channels produce actual customers, not just leads?
SELECT lead_source, ROUND(COUNT(CASE WHEN outcome='Won' THEN 1 END) * 100.0 / COUNT(*), 1) AS conversion_rate FROM leads GROUP BY lead_source ORDER BY conversion_rate DESC;
Results
Query 4 — follow-ups needed right now
Business question: Which leads are at risk of falling through the cracks?
SELECT first_name, last_name, stage, estimated_deal_value, assigned_agent FROM leads WHERE follow_up_needed = TRUE AND outcome = 'Open' ORDER BY estimated_deal_value DESC;

Query 5 — agent performance ranking
Business question: Who is closing the most revenue?
SELECT assigned_agent, COUNT(*) AS deals_won, SUM(estimated_deal_value) AS revenue_closed FROM leads WHERE outcome = 'Won' GROUP BY assigned_agent ORDER BY revenue_closed DESC;
